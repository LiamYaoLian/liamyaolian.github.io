Knowing the principles is different from implementing correctly, so a checklist is critical.

Click each step to see more.

Step 1. Clarify the goal and the work scope first

Trap 1: It is good to be proactive, take initiative, and think outside of the box for the team. However, sometimes taking initiative may backfire. One occasion is that the efforts misalign with the ultimate goal, come outside of the project scope, or unnecessarily increase the workload. The first step should be understanding the big picture – the goal of the team and the organization and the work scope.
